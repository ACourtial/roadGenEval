Welcome to my survey.

The goal of this survey is to evaluate serveral technics to generalize roads shape in map.
It should take you about twenty minutes.

We will present you images of roads generalized via traditional method at IGN (the french national map agency) and images of generalised roads produce by artificial inteligence (AI) and especially deep learning. This approach aims to provide many example of generalization to a deep neural network in order to extract a proposses that directly produce an image of generalisation.

Definition

Map generalisation seeks to adapt a precise geographic dataset for visualisation at a smaller scale. It should provide readable map and conserve the information.
